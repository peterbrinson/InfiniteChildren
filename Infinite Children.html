Infinite Children

Infinite Children is a science fiction fairy tale videogame about a member of the first generation to defer aging. You play the parent.

You shepherd your 8-year old kid, Theo, through a day of trials - helping him battle monsters and dodge pitfalls. If he successfully completes this day of trials, he will be rewarded with an indeterminately long life.

Written by Greg Chandler

Art by Fred F

Theo played by John Prosky

Portal textures based on quilt by Darlynn Evans
